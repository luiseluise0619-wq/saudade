In-app revocation routes

For convenience these are the exact endpoints the app calls when you exercise a right:

  • POST /auth/signout — sign out the current device
  • POST /auth/signout-all — revoke every session and burn unused magic links
  • GET /auth/export — download your data as JSON
  • POST /auth/delete — hard-delete account (irreversible)
  • POST /auth/consent — log a consent grant or revocation per category
