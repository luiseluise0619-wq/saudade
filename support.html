How Saudade is funded.

Saudade is a magazine. It is small and it intends to stay small. The newspaper economy of clicks and outrage is not the economy we want to live in.

Three ways to keep this paper going. The first costs nothing. The other two are how the lights stay on.

Reader

Free.

Today's dispatches. Yesterday's. The full atlas. The listening room. The personal counters. Forever.

No newsletter walls. No pop-ups. No tracking pixels. No ads.

Patron

$3 + / month, your choice

For people who like the paper and want it to keep going. No paywall, no special access — just sustenance for a small operation.

Your name appears in the colophon if you want it to.

Subscriber FORTHCOMING

$5 / month, or $50 / year (when it opens)

The full dispatch archive, indexed by city, searchable, kept open forever. Free readers see the most recent week; subscribers will see all of it.

A printable PDF of the quarterly issue, mailed to your inbox four times a year.

Vote on the next city we open a desk in.

None of this exists yet. The archive needs a year of dispatches first; the quarterly PDF needs the first issue closed; the city-vote needs an audience to vote. Subscriber opens once those three are real, not before. Patron (above) is how to support the paper today.

Why not advertising

The constitution of this magazine forbids it. We do not list a café in the atlas in exchange for money. We do not run banner ads, native ads, sponsored dispatches, affiliate links, or recommendation widgets. The reader's attention is not for sale.

Why not free forever

Because writing a city's news every weekday, three editions in five languages, takes work. Because the listening room and the atlas need maintenance. Because at some point a single person cannot do it alone, and another writer needs paying.

If 1,000 readers send $5 a month, this paper is sustainable. That is the goal. There is no funding round, no investors, no pivot lurking.

What you get for free, in detail

  • Every dispatch from the last 7 days, in your edition's language.
  • The atlas of cafés. Every entry. Forever.
  • The listening room. Photographs, ambient stems, work timer.
  • All counters — Schengen, tax, pension, insurance, stays.
  • The quarterly dispatch supplement.
  • Tour mode. Browse without an account.

What subscribers see additionally

  • The full dispatch archive, beyond seven days.
  • City-by-city search across every dispatch we have filed.
  • Quarterly PDF, A4 portrait, print-ready.
  • A vote on the next desk city.

Cancel anytime

One click in your account page. No phone calls. No retention emails. We will not chase you.
