2. What we don't collect

We do not collect names, phone numbers, addresses, government IDs, biometrics, payment card numbers (Stripe/Toss handle those), or precise geolocation without explicit opt-in.

3. Sharing

Sub-processors: Cloudflare (hosting), Google (Analytics, only with consent), Resend (sign-in email), Stripe / Toss (payments). We never sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising as defined by Cal. Civ. Code §1798.140(ad).

4. Your rights

Under GDPR Arts.15–22, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD Art.18, APPI §28–30, and PIPA §35–37 you may: access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, withdraw consent, object to automated decisions, and opt out of "sale/share" (we do neither).

In-app, instantly: open the Account & Permissions panel (#account) to list active sessions, sign out (this device), sign out everywhere (also burns unused sign-in links), export your data as JSON, delete your account, or revoke any consent category.

By email: luiseluise0619@gmail.com. We answer within PIPA 10 days · GDPR 1 month · CCPA 45 days · LGPD 15 days · APPI "without delay."

5. AI Act §50 disclosure

The Dispatches section uses Cloudflare Workers AI (Llama 3.1 8B) for classification/scoring and Google Gemini 2.0 Flash for rewriting and translation. Each item is labelled AI DRAFT or REWRITTEN. Each edition's JSON carries machine-readable ai_assisted: true + ai_models metadata so external readers and aggregators can detect AI provenance. You can revoke AI consent in #account.

6. Children

Not directed to children under 14 (KR) / 16 (EU) / 13 (US, COPPA) / 13 (BR, LGPD).

7. Retention

  • Sessions — 30 days from last use, or until you revoke them
  • Magic-link tokens — 15 minutes, single-use
  • Analytics — up to 14 months (GA4 default)
  • Payment records — 5 years (Korean e-commerce law)
  • Deletion log — kept (only hashed user_id and email_hash) for audit

8. Security

HTTPS only, Content-Security-Policy, no third-party scripts on the auth path, server-side session revocation, and 72-hour breach notification under GDPR Art.33.

9. Supervisory authorities

  • EU — your local DPA (CNIL, BfDI, AEPD, CNPD, etc.)
  • UK — ICO (ico.org.uk)
  • US (California) — Attorney General (oag.ca.gov/privacy)
  • Brazil — ANPD (gov.br/anpd)
  • Japan — Personal Information Protection Commission (ppc.go.jp)
  • Korea — privacy.go.kr · 1833-6972

10. Endpoints called when you exercise a right

  • POST /auth/signout — sign out this device
  • POST /auth/signout-all — revoke every session, burn unused sign-in links
  • GET /auth/export — JSON of everything we hold
  • POST /auth/delete — hard-delete account
  • POST /auth/consent — log a consent grant or revocation
