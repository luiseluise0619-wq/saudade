## First comment (the maker post)

```
hey PH 👋 LEEJAEJIN here, editor of saudade.

the short version: I am one of the millions of digital nomads who watch four
calendars every morning — visa days, tax-residency days, insurance windows,
pension contributions. every existing tool either focuses on one of those
four, costs $9/month for the rest, or sells my visa data for analytics.

so I built one that does all four for free. then I dressed it up like a
newspaper because I think the productivity-coach voice that most nomad
apps use ("MAXIMISE! OPTIMISE! HACK!") is exhausting in a country whose
public transport you don't yet understand.

what's actually here:
• Schengen 90/180 rolling calculator (the only one I trust — 24 unit
  tests including the open-stay, single-day, and rolling-window edges)
• Tax-residency 183 across 60+ countries with the GB/AU/IN tax-year
  overrides that the textbook calculators forget
• Insurance-gap counter that shows you which weeks you were uninsured
• Pension-month counter for KR-NPS / UK-NIC / PT-SocSeg / etc.
• An "italic moments" layer on the cover — your data turned into
  sentences ("641 days since you last sat in a Seoul café")
• Atom feed, 5 languages, no signup needed to try it

what I didn't build:
• tracking. there's no GA on the cover or auth path; analytics opt-in
  via #account, logged server-side so the lawful basis is auditable
• AI-everything. dispatches are AI-assisted but each item is labelled
  AI DRAFT or REWRITTEN; the AI Act §50 disclosure is machine-readable
  in every JSON edition
• a Pro tier, yet. running cost is low enough that I want to see what
  the affiliate model can carry first

free. no signup needed. open the page, click #demo, see what it looks
like populated. or hit etymology.html to read why a 13th-century
Portuguese word ended up on my masthead.

ask me anything — I read every comment.
```

## What success looks like

- **Top 5 of the day:** plausible if the comment is good and at least one Hunter with reach upvotes early.
- **Top 1 of the day:** unlikely; most "Top 1" launches are funded teams with 30+ supporters in their personal network.
- **Right-tail outcomes:** 50–500 upvotes, 30–80 paid traffic referrals over 7 days, 1–3 inbound press requests.

The actual win is the supporter list and the press hit, not the rank.
