01REWRITTEN

Jacaranda peak is days away.

Avenida Dom Carlos I + Largo do Carmo turn purple by mid-May.

Lisbon's jacaranda bloom typically peaks in the second and third weeks of May. The strongest concentrations: Avenida Dom Carlos I (just south of Príncipe Real), Largo do Carmo, and the slope below São Pedro de Alcântara. The bloom holds three to four weeks before petals begin to drop.

02REWRITTEN

AIMA replaces SEF: D7 / D8 visa pipeline unchanged on paper.

Backlog is the actual issue, not the rules.

AIMA (Agência para a Integração, Migrações e Asilo), which absorbed SEF's residency functions in 2024, continues to handle D7 (passive income) and D8 (digital nomad) renewals. Public guidance documents have not changed in 2026; reported delays are operational, not regulatory. Lisbon area applicants commonly bridge gaps with the temporary residence proof issued on application receipt.
