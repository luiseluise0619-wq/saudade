01REWRITTEN

AFIP confirms peso-blue ambiguity in nomad reporting.

Foreign income converted at official rate, not blue.

The Argentine tax agency clarified in a public Q&A that foreign-source income earned by tax-resident nomads must be reported at the official ARS rate, regardless of the parallel-market premium. The blue-dollar gap is not honoured.
