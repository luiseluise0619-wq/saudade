El Poblado tightens short-let registration.

Owners must register by July or face fines.

The Medellín municipality's tourism office requires all short-stay rental operators in El Poblado to register on the city's short-let portal by July 1. Registration is free; non-registration carries a COP 2M fine.

01REWRITTEN

Bairro Alto noise ordinance widens to weeknights.

Outdoor music ends at 23h00 Mon–Thu starting June.

A new municipal noise ordinance extends the existing weekend cap to weeknights. Tasca-style outdoor patios in Bairro Alto and Bica must end amplified music by 23h00 from Monday to Thursday from June 1.

Câmara Municipal · Diário da República · 2026-04-30

02AI DRAFT

D7 visa renewals slow at the SEF in May.

Two-month wait for the in-person interview, by appointment only.

Service desks at the Serviço de Estrangeiros e Fronteiras have been booked through July for D7 renewals. Online pre-interviews are accepted only for second-time renewals. New applicants should expect three desk visits over four months.

“Three desk visits, four months. Be early.” — Editor

NHR 2.0 frozen at the same rate through 2027.

The 20 % flat for new highly-qualified arrivals stays.

AT (Autoridade Tributária) confirmed in writing that NHR 2.0, the post-2024 successor to NHR, will keep its 20 % flat band for IFICI-classified arrivals through fiscal year 2027.
