Revenue Department clarifies remitted-income rule.

Income earned in 2026 and remitted in 2027 is taxable.

A revenue ruling published this week reaffirms that foreign-sourced income earned by a Thai tax resident is taxable when remitted, regardless of the year earned. Remittance triggers the tax event.
